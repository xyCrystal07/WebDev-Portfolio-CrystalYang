Design Thinking Process

How I applied design thinking principles to create this portfolio website

1

Empathize

I started by researching what potential employers and clients look for in a portfolio. I interviewed several web developers and reviewed numerous portfolios to understand user needs.

2

Define

Based on my research, I identified key requirements: showcase projects effectively, demonstrate technical skills, highlight design thinking, and ensure accessibility.

3

Ideate

I brainstormed multiple layout options, color schemes, and navigation structures. I created several wireframes to explore different approaches to presenting my work.

4

Prototype

I developed a working prototype focusing on mobile-first design. I implemented the core functionality and tested different interactive elements.

5

Test

I conducted usability tests with peers and mentors, gathering feedback on navigation, content clarity, and visual appeal. I iterated based on this feedback.

Key Takeaways

  • User-centered design leads to more effective portfolios
  • Iteration is crucial for refining both design and content
  • Accessibility should be considered from the beginning
  • Clear navigation is essential for positive user experience
